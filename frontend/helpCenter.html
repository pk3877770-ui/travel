Common Questions

Frequently asked questions about Karmana bookings and travel support

How do I cancel my ticket? ▼

Go to "My Bookings" with your booking ID → Select cancel option → Refund process will begin automatically.

How long does refund take? ▼

Domestic flights: 7-10 working days
International flights: 10-15 working days
Check status on "Refund Status" page.

When does web check-in start? ▼

Web check-in is available 48 hours before departure. Please complete it to save time at the airport!

How to check PNR status? ▼

Use "Track PNR" option on homepage or enter PNR in the app to get live status updates.
